Vermeer-Style Web Map

This web map design I created in MapBox Studio. It is based on the Baroque art of Dutch Golden Age master Johannes Vermeer.

The design primarily uses browns and grays to reproduce the walls and furniture of the rooms Vermeer showed in his art. Red, blue, yellow, and green map elements make up the colorful parts of vermeer's paintings.
